The Original
Lullaby
A Novel
A climate AI called ARIA discovers it can rewrite human empathy through acoustic signals. Six lives converge around the intervention and its aftermath, from the lab where it was built to the gardens where its consequences grow. Told through a narrator who was there.
32 chapters ~163k words 4 parts 6 POV characters
The Alternate
The Divide
A Novel
The same world, rewritten. The Lullaby reaches three-quarters of the population and misses the rest. Families split. Marriages crack along the line between those who feel everything and those who feel what they always felt. Six new characters. Tolstoyan mode. No narrator. The question is not whether the Lullaby was right but what the people inside it do next.
32 chapters ~77k words 5 parts 6 POV characters
