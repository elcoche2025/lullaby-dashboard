Novels
The Original
Lullaby
A Novel
A climate AI called ARIA rewrites human empathy through acoustic signals on a single night. The truth is buried. The world calls it the Great Awakening. Six lives converge around the secret — the architect who can’t tell his daughter, the philosopher who can feel but not prove, the father from the control zone who kills to protect a lie, the engineer who deletes the only evidence. Buried-truth structure. R6+ revision integrates Frankfurt, Murdoch, Callard, Korsgaard, Parfit, Borges, Chiang, and disability rights theory into the philosophical architecture.
33 chapters ~165k words 4 parts + prologue 6 POV strands R6+ current revision
Book Two — In Progress
The Seam
A Novel (replacing The Divide)
Thirty years after the Lullaby, the first generation born without it is coming of age. A doctoral student at Hopkins named Noor Halabi notices her generation’s empathy is different — cognitive where the older generation’s is somatic, chosen where theirs is involuntary. Her investigation pulls her backward through neuroscience, philosophy, and a dead man’s notebook in Idaho. The novel never confirms what she suspects. Dual timeline: Noor (future, investigation) + Maren (past, philosophical crisis). Grounded in Frankfurt, Murdoch, Callard, Thaler/Sunstein, disability rights theory, and the neuroscience of mirror neurons.
~10k words (standalone novella complete) 32 chapters planned ~90k target Dual timeline
